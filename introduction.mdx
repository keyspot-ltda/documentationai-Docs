## Overview

Keyspot provides real estate agencies with a unified platform that combines CRM functionality, AI-enhanced websites, and intelligent lead management. You centralize leads, properties, and customer interactions in one place, while the AI works around the clock to convert visitors into qualified opportunities. This setup streamlines your operations and increases conversions without the complexity of multiple tools.
