## Overview

Group and order documentation pages in your Keyspot project.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Project">
    Go to your project dashboard and select the project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add or reorder pages">
    Use the sidebar to drag pages into order or click "Add Page" for new docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Collections">
    Group related pages: click "New Collection" and drag pages inside.
  </Step>
</Steps>
