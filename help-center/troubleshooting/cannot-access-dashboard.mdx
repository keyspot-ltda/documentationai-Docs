---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Resolve dashboard access problems in Keyspot.
---

## Symptoms

- Dashboard does not load
- Receive 403 or login loop

## Causes

- Logged out session
- Browser cache issues

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in again">
    Fully log out, then log back in at `https://app.keyspot.io`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear your browser's cache/cookies and reload the page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
