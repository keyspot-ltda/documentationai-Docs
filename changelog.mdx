## v1.2.0

<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v1.2.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## New Features

- Added AI-powered property recommendations on the site vitrine, matching visitor preferences in real-time.
- Introduced custom tags for imóveis (properties) in the CRM, with visual filters for better organization.

## Improvements

- Optimized CRM lead centralization, reducing load times by `>30%` for large real estate portfolios.
- Enhanced site SEO with MCP Web support for AI agents to recommend your listings.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed video feed rendering issues in the immersive site experience.
- Resolved attendance tracking sync errors between site forms and CRM.

</Update>

## v1.1.0

<Update label="2024-09-20" description="v1.1.0" tags={["feature", "bugfix"]}>

## New Features

- Launched IA modo (AI mode) for personalized property searches, like "Apartamento com 3 quartos".
- Added empreendimentos (developments) pages with galleries, floor plans, and direct conversion tools.

## Improvements

- Upgraded lead qualification with intelligent scoring based on visitor behavior.

## Bug Fixes

- Corrected favoritos (favorites) personalization sync across devices.
- Patched filters avançados (advanced filters) real-time update delays.

</Update>

## v1.0.0

<Update label="2024-08-10" description="v1.0.0" tags={["feature", "breaking"]}>

## Initial Release

- Core CRM for centralizing leads, imóveis, and atendimento (support).
- Complete site builder with IA integration for converting visitors to clients.
- Vitrine inteligente (smart showcase), feed de vídeos (video feed), and exclusive designs.

## Breaking Changes

- Initial schema updates for property data; migrate existing data via dashboard.

</Update>

## Upgrade Instructions

Follow these steps to update your Keyspot instance:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Compatibility" icon="check-circle">

    Review the release notes above for breaking changes.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Backup Data" icon="database">

    Export leads and imóveis from the dashboard.

    ```
    # Via dashboard: Settings > Data Export
    ```

  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply Update" icon="zap">

    In your dashboard, navigate to **Updates** and click **Upgrade Now**.

  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Features" icon="search">

    Test AI recommendations and CRM sync.

  </Step>
</Steps>

- Video feed max 10 items per page; use pagination for larger galleries.

</Expandable>
